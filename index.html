If you want to change the world, start with yourself.

ArtCube TM is a company dedicated to working on yourself and self-improvement. In addition to our main direction, you can find a lot of useful information.

At www.startsfromyou.com you can find specially designed courses. They have answers to a wide variety of topics.

From stress and depression to problems of making money and creating a family.

Why do we work with such diverse topics and how are they interrelated?

Understand the causes, not the effects.

All of our problems depend on ourselves. We create them ourselves and ultimately solve them ourselves. However, most problems should in principle not appear. Our task and goal is to show people the causes of their problems. It does not matter what they are. It can be psycho-emotional state, family, work or business, health. It does not matter at all.

Everything in life is interconnected.

Man - his life and essence - is the result of cause and effect. Our whole life, in fact, consists of cause and effect. We give answers to the right questions. We work on causes and establish consequences. Helping dreams become reality.

Do you feel you have potential?

Very often people feel the potential in themselves, but do not understand how to unlock it. Or feel psycho-emotional stress, but do not understand how to avoid it. ArtCube TM company aims at self-improvement of a person. Our goal is to give the right information as coherently and accurately as possible. Our own developments in the field of human self-improvement will help to open any person. And the field of activity is not important, because it is about self-improvement.

If you want to do something well, do it yourself.

To know everything around you, you must first know yourself. Many people know this phrase, but not everyone really understands it. It is working on yourself that can turn your life around. With the right approach, the same work can be done with different efficiency. It's the same with psychology, relationships, health and other areas of life.
